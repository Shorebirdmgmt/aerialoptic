About Aerial Optic

We Capture Stunning Aerial Images That Tell Your Story

At Aerial Optic, we believe that every picture tells a story. As an FAA Part 107 certified drone pilot based in Kingsville, Maryland, we specialize in cinematic aerial photography and videography for marine, real estate, construction, events, and commercial clients. Whether chasing sportfishing boats offshore or showcasing a waterfront property from above, we deliver powerful visuals that elevate your brand.
